Privacy Policy

: This Privacy Policy outlines how personal information and data will be collected, used, and protected in the context of the "Folkverse Venture" Android Application developed for Grade 5 Filipino classes at Lawang Bato Elementary School.

  1. Information Collection

    1. Personal Information:The Application may collect and store limited personal information of students, teachers, and administrators for the purpose of providing access to educational content and tracking progress. This information may include names, student IDs, and contact details.
    2. Usage Data:The Application may collect usage data, such as user interactions and progress within the Application. This data is used to enhance the learning experience.
    3. Cookies:The Application may use cookies to enhance user experience. Users have the option to disable cookies in their browser settings.

  2. Information Use

    1. Educational Purposes::The personal information collected will be used solely for educational purposes, including tracking student progress, providing access to educational materials, and evaluating the effectiveness of the Application.
    2. Communication:Contact information may be used to facilitate communication between teachers, students, and parents. This includes sending updates, notifications, and feedback requests.
    3. Improvement:Usage data and feedback may be used to improve the Application's functionality, content, and user experience.

  3. Data Security

    1. Security Measures:We employ industry-standard security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.
    2. Data Access:Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only, and data is stored on secure servers.

  4. Data Sharing

    1. Third-Party Services:We may use third-party services to assist with data processing, analytics, and communication. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and security measures.
    2. Legal Obligations:We may disclose personal information in response to legal requirements, court orders, or government requests.

  5. Data Retention

    1. We will retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes mentioned in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Afterward, data will be securely deleted.

  6. Consent and Control

    1. By using the Application, users consent to the collection and use of their information as described in this Privacy Policy. Users have the right to review, modify, or delete their personal information upon request.

  7. Changes to Privacy Policy

    1. This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of any significant changes. Continued use of the Application after these changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  8. Contact Information

    1. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Contact Us Page
